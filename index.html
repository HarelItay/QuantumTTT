How to Play Quantum Tic-Tac-Toe

🌀 Quantum Superposition

Quantum pieces exist in both X and O states simultaneously until observed!

🎯 Wave Function Collapse

When you collapse a quantum piece, it randomly becomes either X or O based on its probability!

🏆 How to Win

Get three collapsed pieces (real X's or O's) in a row - horizontally, vertically, or diagonally!

🎮 Game Actions

  • Select a probability card and click an empty cell to place a quantum piece
  • Click on any quantum piece to collapse it (instead of placing a new piece)
  • Watch the magic happen!

🎉 Quantum Victory! 🎉

Fun Fact: In real quantum mechanics, measuring a quantum system causes it to collapse into a definite state, just like in our game!
